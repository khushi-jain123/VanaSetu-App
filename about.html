About Vanasetu

Vanasetu is a platform dedicated to promoting tree adoption and environmental sustainability. Our goal is to connect individuals, communities, and organizations to take part in reforestation efforts and contribute to a greener planet.

Our Mission

We strive to make tree adoption easier and accessible for everyone. By partnering with various nurseries and NGOs, we ensure that every adopted tree is nurtured and maintained for a sustainable future.

Why Choose Us?

- Easy online tree adoption process.
- Verified nurseries and NGOs for plant care.
- Commitment to long-term sustainability.
- Monthly updates on tree growth.
